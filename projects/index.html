Projects

Decryptr

A digital port of the board game Decrypto which I built to play while social distancing.

Sponsorhub

A marketplace for game developers to connect with streamers and youtubers to produce sponsored streams. Now sadly defunct - I should really do a post-mortem write up at some point.

Hairy Little Buggers

Hairy Little Buggers is a Simulation-Management game about cheeky Dwarves. Protect your dwarves as they dig tunnels, craft tools & fight battles. Watch as they build workshops & bedrooms, farms & great halls! Train your warriors & protect against goblins!

Runway Calculator

A little tool that connected to your bank account via Plaid and enabled you to explore different fiscal scenarios (like calculating the amount of runway you have left under different revenue growth assumptions). Not as deserving of a post-mortem as Sponsorhub, but I should do it anyway.
